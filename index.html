About Inuka Afrika Properties Limited

Your trusted partner in real estate investment

Inuka Afrika Properties Limited (IAPL) is a legally registered limited liability company with its head office located in Nyali, Mombasa. IAPL core business is in real estate mainly, land and affordable housing majorly focused in the coastal Kenya and in 2019 moved to Nairobi area with properties and projects in the outskirts of Nairobi.

IAPL offers a complete package of affordable real estate investment solutions, by ensuring they meet customers' property and land ownership from sales and rentals, retail and commercial to mortgage, new development marketing, property management and title issuance.
