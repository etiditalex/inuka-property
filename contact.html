Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What documents do I need to buy land?

You'll need a valid ID (National ID or Passport), proof of income, and we'll guide you through the title deed process. All our properties come with genuine title deeds.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans with no interest. You can start with as little as KES 100,000 and pay the rest in installments.

How long does title deed processing take?

Our title deed processing is fast and efficient, typically taking 3-6 months depending on the property location and documentation requirements.

Can I visit the properties before buying?

Absolutely! We encourage site visits. You can book a site visit through our website or call us directly to arrange a viewing.
