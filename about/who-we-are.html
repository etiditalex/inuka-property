Our Story

Founded in 2010, Inuka Afrika Properties Limited emerged from a vision to transform the real estate landscape in Kenya and beyond. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most trusted property development companies.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every family deserves access to quality, affordable housing and investment opportunities. Over the years, we've remained true to this vision while adapting to the evolving needs of our clients and the market.

Today, we stand as a testament to what can be achieved through dedication, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our portfolio spans across multiple counties in Kenya, with projects that have transformed communities and created lasting value for our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading real estate development company in East Africa, known for innovation, quality, and creating sustainable communities that enrich lives.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional real estate solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients while contributing to the economic and social development of our communities.
